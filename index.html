OUR FACILITIES

THE ROOMS

There are 72 rooms over two Levels at Florence consisting of 30 single and 21 double Rooms all with ensulte. The rooms can cater for individuals or couples. With 32 beds on the ground floor and 40 on the first floor, the design provides a safe and secure environment. Each of the rooms are air Conditioned, have private Wardrobes and Bedroom furniture provided. The furniture includes a fully adjustable Hydraulic bed, an over bed table and bed side locker with a lockable drawer for personal items. On site cleaning ensures the facility is kept clean and tidy At all times. In addition, the laundry is located on site and residents Personal clothing is laundered and returned within 48 Hours. Labelling services are available on request for all Resident clothing.

COMMUNAL AREAS

There are two-day rooms for Residents which include large Screen televisions, Comfortable couches and Chairs. In addition, there are three sun rooms throughout the facility providing quiet areas for residents, friends and family to meet and enjoy. The facility also houses a “Quiet Room” which can be Used for prayer, Contemplation or time for solitude.

THE GARDEN

Surrounding the facility is a Number of landscaped Garden areas providing light And sun filled areas for Relaxation and rest. Raised garden beds also Allow residents with an Interest in gardening to Continue to pursue their Desire. If needed assistance From activities staff will be Provided. In addition, there are car Parks on site to provide safe And secure car parking for Visitors and stuff to use.
